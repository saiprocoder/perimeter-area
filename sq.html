Square

Square is a quadrilateral which has 4 sides and 4 vertices. It's all sides are equal.


Square's Perimeter

As you all know that all the sides in a square are equal. So perimeter of a square = side + side + side + side. But you all know that repeated addition is multiplication. So, perimeter of a square is = 4 × side.


Square's Area

The Square's area is the area covered by it. The formula of finding a square's area = side × side.
