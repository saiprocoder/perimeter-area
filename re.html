Rectangle

Rectangle is a quadrilateral which has 4 sides and 4 vertices. It's opposite sides are equal.


Rectangle's Perimeter

Rectangle has a length and breadth. There are 2 lengths and breadths. Perimeter = l + b + l + b. So it becomes 2 × l + 2 × b. We can write it as 2 × (l + b).


Rectangle's Area

The Rectangle's area is the space covered by it. The formula is l × b.


The Formula of Rectangle's Perimeter

The formula of rectangle's perimeter = 2 × (l + b).


The Formula of Rectangle's Area

The formula of rectangle's area = l × b.
