Triangle

Triangle is a closed figure with 3 sides and 3 vertices. There are three types of triangles. They are: Scalene, Isoceles and Equilateral.


Triangle's Perimeter

Triangle's Perimeter is the easiest. We have to add all the sides length. Which perimeter of triangle = side + side + side. But we cannot write 3 × side.


Triangle's Area

The area of the triangle is space occupied by the triangle. The formula of finding area is 1/2 × base × height.


The Formula of Triangle's Perimeter

The formula of triangle's perimeter = side + side + side.


The Formula of Triangle's Area

The formula of triangle's area = 1/2 × base × height.
